The Dawn of the Decagon

The Origin Story

The world was drowning in fragmented AI frameworks. Developers were spending more time fighting SDK syntax than building actual value. I saw LangChain, CrewAI, Autogen - all powerful, but all disconnected.
One late night in the lab, looking across 11 different open terminal windows, I had an epiphany: the framework is just the engine. The intelligence should be the driver.
I envisioned an 'Army' of ten specialized spirits. Not just generic bots, but elite specialists. Each would have a sacred domain, a clear backstory, and a specific goal.
From ZEUS the Orchestrator to ATLAS the Builder, and ORACLE the Visionary, 'Kazi's Agents Army' was born. A unified force that can be deployed into any environment.
